About Us

Meet the Founder

Whitney Wall

Whitney started Waymaker Co. in her small two bedroom apartment in 2021 selling Christian themed stickers and apparel. She wanted to praise and worship her Jesus in all that she did, but realized that she also held passions for things outside of the gospel as well. Whitney kept the name of Waymaker Co., which pointed to Jesus, but decided to branch out in her love of graphic design and hand crafting items.

Since then, Whitney has

  • Designed and published journals, along with other stationary
  • Created various styles of keychains
  • Promoted mental health awareness with affirmation cards

Whitney enjoys spending time with her husband and little boy. You'll often find her and her little family outside enjoying hiking, fishing, camping, four-wheeling, and more. When given the time, Whitney loves to read! Follow her on goodreads for updates!

Our Mission

Mental Health Advocacy

Battling with mental illness is increasing prevalent in todays world. Waymaker Co. understands this and hopes to crush the stigma and promote healthy habits. Whitney, the founder and owner, has battled depression and anxiety and understands this on a personal level. With each purchase, 5% of the proceedings are donated to local Utah mental health charities and nonprofits.
